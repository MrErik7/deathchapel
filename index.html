Blackened Death Metal

About the band:

Death Chapel is a Gothenburg based Swedish death metal band. They are quite young as the age of the members range between 17 and 19. The band has only been around a bit over a year, as it started in the fall of 2021.

Why do they do it?

The purpose of the band is to make good, entertaining death metal. They want to break the stereotypes surrounding the genre, metal does not have to be serious to be good.

When performing live, Death Chapel focuses on making fun, comedic and satanic shows. They would rather have fun than always play the right notes, they want the audience to feel welcomed and understand the music. After one of death chapels infamous mosh pits, participants are expected to be sent to their graves laughing.

Members:

Erik Gustafsson

Born in a goat family, he was first introduced to society at the age of 6 when he was put to a trial of blood. Having suffered many amputations, setbacks and deaths, whilst jerking off Erik got a vision for a great band which he and the drummer Sode started up, they teamed up with a guitarist by the name of Isaac. Shortly thereafter the infamous Gabriel Sepulveda joined the band.

Gabriel Sepulveda

Gabriel was born on a cold, dark winter night on July the 5th. After escaping the great plague, the upcoming winter almost took his life. When hearing the electric banana band for the first time combined with 10 tabs of acid, he started playing guitar. After that he was hooked, after meeting the lead singer in the local satanic church he was introduced to the band Death Chapel.
