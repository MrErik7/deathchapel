About Death Chapel

Death Chapel is a Gothenburg based Swedish death metal band. They are quite young as the age of the members range between 17 and 19. The band has only been around a bit over a year, as it started in the fall of 2021.

Wictor Bereståhl

Born in the depths of the mountains surrounded by primordial steel he fashioned those metals into strings. Breaking his way out of the dark stone with a riff so metal it cracked the mountain in half now free he found a band.. Death chapel!

Icons to social media here:

Erik Gustafsson

Born in a goat family, he was first introduced to society at the age of six when he was put to a trial of blood. Having suffered many amputations, setbacks and deaths, whilst jerking off Erik got a vision for a great band which he and the drummer Sode started up, they teamed up with a guitarist by the name of Isaac. Shortly thereafter the infamous Gabriel Sepulveda joined the band.

Gabriel Sepulveda

Gabriel was born on a cold, dark winter night on July the fifth. After escaping the great plague, the upcoming winter almost took his life. When hearing the electric banana band for the first time combined with ten tabs of acid, he started playing guitar. After that he was hooked, after meeting the lead singer in the local satanic church he was introduced to the band Death Chapel.
