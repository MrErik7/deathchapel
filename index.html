About Death Chapel

Death Chapel, a death metal entity rooted in the heart of Gothenburg, Sweden, is a young band on the rise. Established in the autumn of 2021, the band has since then released two EPs. Drawing inspiration from the iconic "Gothenburg sound" and influenced by the likes of In Flames, At The Gates, and Gojira, their music is a fusion of melodic, death, groove, and progressive metal.

Not content with conventional boundaries, Death Chapel infuses elements of satire, horror, and comedy into both their live performances and compositions. The band extends an invitation to audiences to delve into the dark recesses of their twisted and sick minds, as reflected in their music.

Gabriel Sepulveda

Gabriel Sepulveda was born on a cold, dark winter night in July. After running away from Chile, escaping the great Mexican plague, he made it to Sweden. But being an outcast surely took its toll and Gabriel soon ended up on the streets. But when hearing the electric banana band for the first time whilst on 10 tabs of acid, he got inspired to start playing guitar. After that he was hooked, he met the lead singer in the local satanic church and was then introduced to the band Death Chapel.

Leon Olsbo

But after years of captivity Leon finally broke loose, running away to live with the homeless. There he found death chapel, and fell in love.
